Muddslide

The Muddslide is a downhill bike designed for gnarly gravity riding. From bike park days with friends to World Cup downhill races, you can hit big jumps and charge rocky lines on the Muddslide.

Adobe

Adobe is where race-worthy mountain bikes begin. The smooth, lightweight RockShox fork and upgraded parts make this model the best choice for new riders who want a fast cross country hardtail that will hold its own against our higher-end race bikes.

Mudd Pit

Mudd Pit bikes boast all-rounder trail geometry with moderate chainstay and wheelbase lengths combined with a relaxed head angle; these bikes feel agile in twisty trails while ensuring confidence at high speed.
